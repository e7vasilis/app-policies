Privacy Policy for ATC Sync

Effective Date: April 25, 2026

ATC Sync ("the Application") is a coordination tool for Air Traffic Controllers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Profile Data: We collect your Name, Email Address, Username (Callsign), and Profile Picture provided via Google OAuth to allow other controllers to identify and connect with you.
  • Schedule Data: We store the shift schedules you "paint" or generate within the app to calculate overlaps with your connections.
  • Usage Data: We use PostHog to collect anonymized technical data (device type, pages visited) to improve app performance and troubleshoot errors.

2. Use of Google User Data

ATC Sync requests access to your Google Calendar (via the auth/calendar scope). This access is used for the following strictly limited purpose:

Dedicated Synchronization: We use this permission exclusively to create and manage a dedicated secondary calendar named "ATC Sync". This allows your work shifts to be visible on your devices without modifying, reading, or cluttering your primary personal calendar.

We do not read, delete, or modify any events on your primary or other personal calendars.

3. Google API Disclosure (Limited Use)

ATC Sync's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We share information only with the following service providers necessary for app functionality:

  • Supabase: For secure database storage and authentication.
  • PostHog: For product analytics and session recording for technical support.

5. Your Rights: The "Hard Delete"

We believe in your right to be forgotten. ATC Sync provides a Delete Account feature in the application settings. Triggering this action will result in the immediate and permanent erasure of your profile, all shift history, and all integration tokens from our servers.
